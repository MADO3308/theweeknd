02 / PROFILE

The Artist
Abel Tesfaye

Abel Makkonen Tesfaye, known professionally as The Weeknd, is a multi-platinum, diamond-certified global superstar whose cinematic vision has redefined modern pop and R&B music.

From his anonymous, genre-shifting mixtapes in 2011 to headlining the Super Bowl Halftime Show and breaking historic chart records, his dark, atmospheric production and soaring falsetto continue to dominate global culture.
